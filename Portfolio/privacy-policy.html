Privacy Policy

How I handle information on my portfolio website

Overview

This privacy policy explains how I collect and use information when you visit my portfolio website. I value your privacy and aim to be transparent about my data practices.

Information Collection

When you use the contact form on my website, I collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any information you provide in your message

My website also automatically collects some technical data like your IP address and browser type through standard web analytics.

How I Use Your Information

I only use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Communicate with you about potential collaborations or opportunities
  • Improve my website

I will never sell your personal information to third parties or use it for marketing purposes without your consent.

Contact Form

My contact form uses Formspree to process submissions. When you submit the form, your information is also processed according to Formspree's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what information I have about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Object to my use of your information

If you have any privacy concerns, please contact me directly.

Contact Information
