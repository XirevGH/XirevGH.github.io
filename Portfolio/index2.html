About Me

Crafting Worlds, One Line of Code at a Time

Ever since I typed my first `print("Hello World")`, I've been captivated by the power of code to create new realities. My journey into game development started with a simple desire: to build the kinds of immersive, engaging worlds I loved exploring as a player.

I thrive on the challenges of game programming, from optimizing complex systems in C++ for Unreal Engine to architecting elegant solutions in C# for Unity. I'm passionate about performance, clean code, and collaborative problem-solving. Whether it's developing core gameplay mechanics, implementing intricate AI, or building robust multiplayer features, I approach every task with dedication and a drive for excellence.

Beyond the code, I'm a lifelong learner, constantly exploring new technologies and design principles to push my creative boundaries. I am also a hardcore gamer, going as far to earning a spot on the Guinness World Records website and an interview with Red Bull. Let's build something amazing together!
